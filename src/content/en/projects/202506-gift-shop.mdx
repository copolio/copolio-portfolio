## Overview

Developed the backend system for Karrot Pay's Gift Shop service. Built a B2B/B2C gift card platform for stable promotion and reward operations, and improved entry and payment accessibility through payment method expansion and chatroom integration.

## Key Responsibilities

### B2B Gift Card Platformization
- Restructured B2B/B2C policies and data structures, organized gift card lifecycle (status, expiration, settlement)
- Expanded internal APIs and operational features to enable stable promotion and reward operations
- Enhanced operations with gift voucher usage data pipeline

### Payment Method Expansion & Chatroom Integration
- Added card payment as a payment method to improve payment accessibility
- Handled settlement policy changes for card payment adoption
- Implemented seamless integration from Karrot Alba chatrooms to Gift Shop (chatroom-based entry, events, message flow)

## Results

- Established B2B gift card foundation enabling 1%+ larger discounts compared to retail prices
- Improved Gift Shop entry and payment accessibility through chatroom integration and payment method expansion
