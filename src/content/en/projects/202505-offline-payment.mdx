## Overview

Designed, developed, and operated the backend system for Karrot Pay's offline payment service. Supporting QR/barcode-based Karrot Pay payments at offline stores, covering prepaid settlement, brand promotions, and coupon-based combined payments.

## Key Responsibilities

### Core Payment System Design & Development
- Designed and implemented QR/barcode payment (card company integration), approval/refund APIs
- Built end-to-end payment flows including acquisition/settlement processing and point-based combined payments
- Developed nearby merchant infrastructure with coordinate-based clustering and performance optimization
- Designed settlement correction, differential settlement, and business-day-based payout systems

### Brand Promotion System & Growth
- Built scalable promotion schemes supporting 10+ concurrent brand partnership promotions
- Implemented time-deal, budget management, and benefit exposure features within service architecture
- Expanded entry points and exposure surfaces (home top bar, bottom sheet banners, nearby stores, merchant details)
- Resolved budget concurrency issues, added burn-rate alerts, and handled payment failure/cancellation edge cases

### Merchant Management & Operations
- Enhanced brand-level merchant promotion operations and brand management
- Organized brand-specific QR/barcode availability policies at brand and merchant levels
- Expanded admin features (banner/policy/merchant management, transaction history/CSV export, copy management)
- Built foundation for offline card payment support

### Coupon-based Combined Payment & Additional Features
- Designed and implemented coupon-based combined payment (coupon application on approval, failure/cancellation handling)
- Developed year-end tax summary preview reflecting offline payment usage
- Built card company income deduction data pipeline and file generation batch

## Results

- Successfully launched and stably operated the offline payment service
- Increased daily Karrot Pay signups by 1.5x through brand promotion campaign
- New offline payment user acquisition +104% (vs. prior period)
- Expanded repeat usage base: non-promotion users with 2+ payments grew +114%
