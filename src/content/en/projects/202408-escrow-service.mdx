## Overview

Responsible for operational stabilization and growth of Karrot's escrow payment service after nationwide launch. Identified user pain points through CS data and funnel metrics, and improved transaction conversion and user experience through feature expansion including card payments and shipment tracking.

## Key Responsibilities

### Conversion Improvement & User Experience Enhancement
- Identified user drop-off points based on CS inflow and funnel metrics
- Improved conversion and operational responsiveness through chat message, acceptance flow, policy verification, and admin enhancements
- Developed card payment feature for escrow transactions, expanding payment experience and laying groundwork for transaction volume growth

### Shipment Tracking
- Implemented shipment registration and delivery status tracking based on SweetTracker solution
- Built delivery completion notification flow to improve purchase confirmation rates
- Developed courier management admin for rapid CS response

### Refundability Verification & Card Payment
- Developed refundability product detection to enhance transaction safety
- Implemented card payment within escrow payment flow

- Escrow active buyers +122%, comparing Dec '24 to May '25
